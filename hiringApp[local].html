Venture Engineering and Science

Venture is a summer camp that brings the excitement of engineering to life. Our curriculum develops problem solving skills and engages kids through experiments, hands-on projects, and fun, education-based activities. Our male and female instructors are role models as people, engineers and as scientists. Our diverse group breaks down stereotypes and shows students that anyone who is passionate about engineering, science and technology can pursue it. We know it is critical to develop students’ interest in science early on and to keep them interested. Venture’s curriculum does just that.

For more information, please refer to our website: McMaster Engineering Youth Programs - Venture

Learning Enrichment Advancement Program

Combine your creativity and passion for engineering: become an instructor for the Learning Enrichment Advancement Program. L.E.A.P. is a summer program for high school students from all over Canada and the world. These students work with undergrads (like yourself), graduate students, and professors to undergo university labs, design challenges, and industry tours. As an instructor, you will inspire and lead the students through a curriculum you develop.


For more information, please refer to our website: McMaster Engineering Youth Programs - LEAP
